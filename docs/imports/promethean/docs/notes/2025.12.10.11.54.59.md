## Frames

1. **Library / archive frame** – Names like **Loremesh**, **Mneme**, **Lorelattice** frame the graph as a *living library* or archive of concepts and docs.
2. **Field / organism frame** – Names like **Noofield**, **Mycelia**, **Egregore** frame it as a *growing organism or field*, closer to your Eidolon idea.
3. **Math / infra frame** – Names like **Octagraph**, **Lorelattice**, **Eidonet** frame it as *infrastructure* or *core engine*, which plays well with dev tooling and packages.

---

## Countermoves

For each frame, ways to avoid getting boxed in:

* **Library frame** → Don’t let it sound static. Pair it with verbs like “grow”, “evolve”, “rewire”, not just “store” or “index”.
* **Field/organism frame** → Avoid making it sound mystical to the point of unusable; balance with very boring, concrete subcomponent names.
* **Math/infra frame** → Avoid making it feel like a niche internal tool; give it one human-readable tagline everywhere you show it (e.g., “Octagraph: the brain behind the Loremesh”).
